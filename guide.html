🏠 Welcome to Brainrot Island!

Brainrot Island is a multiplayer tycoon game where you claim a base, collect brainrot creatures, earn cash, buy upgrades, and fight other players for their loot!

Brainrots spawn across 11 unique zones and generate cash passively once deposited at your base. Higher-tier zones have rarer and more valuable brainrots, but you'll need to unlock them first.

🚀 Getting Started

Your first few minutes in Brainrot Island set the pace for everything. Here's what to do:

  1. Claim a base — Walk to an unclaimed base pad and step on it. This is your home where you deposit brainrots and store cash. See Bases.
  2. Explore Beach or Meadow — These two zones are free and always unlocked. Brainrots spawn here every 30 seconds.
  3. Walk over a brainrot to pick it up — You can only carry one at a time. Carry it back to your base deposit zone.
  4. Deposit at your base — Walk into the deposit area to drop it off. The brainrot is now yours and earns cash passively!
  5. Collect your cash — Your brainrots generate $/s. Watch the HUD to see your income growing, then collect it.
  6. Spend cash at the Marketplace — Buy zones, weapons, gear, floors, and consumables.
💡 Pro tip: Don't carry a brainrot for too long! Other players can kill you and you'll drop what you're carrying. Deposit frequently.
🏗 Bases

Bases are your personal territory. Each base has a deposit zone where you drop off brainrots and floors that expand your brainrot storage capacity.

Claiming a Base

Walk onto any unclaimed base pad to claim it. There are 10 bases in the game — one per player. Bases are session-based: when you leave the room, your base becomes available for the next player.

Base Floors

Each base starts with Floor 1 (10 brainrot slots). Unlock additional floors at the Upgrades shop to hold more brainrots and earn more $/s.

Depositing Brainrots

Walk into your base's deposit zone while carrying a brainrot. The brainrot is added to your collection and immediately starts generating $/s. You can check your collection in the Inventory.

🧠 Brainrots

Brainrots are collectible creatures that generate $/s (cash per second) once deposited at your base. There are 99 unique brainrots spread across 11 zones.

How Spawning Works
  • Each zone has a spawn timer that fires every ~30 seconds
  • A random brainrot from that zone is chosen to spawn
  • Maximum 3 brainrots can be active per zone at once
  • Walk over a brainrot to pick it up — you can only carry one at a time

You can collect multiple copies of the same brainrot. Each copy generates cash independently. Stacking common brainrots early is a solid income strategy!

What Happens on Death
  • Lose 50% of your wallet cash — the killer steals it as a bounty
  • Lose all uncollected pending cash — cash that was accumulating but not yet collected is wiped
  • Drop your carried brainrot — if you were carrying one, it's gone
  • Teleport to your base — or to the hub if you don't have a base
💡 Protect your cash: Use the Vault to store cash safely. Vaulted cash is immune to death penalties!

🛒 Shops

The Marketplace is located in the center of the map. Walk into any shop stall to open it. There are four shops:

Upgrades Shop

Buy zone unlocks and base floors. This is where most of your early cash goes. Zones require specific floors to be unlocked first.

Weapons Shop

Buy Pistol, Rifle, or Shotgun. Each weapon has different damage, speed, and range trade-offs. You can own all three and switch between them.

Gear Shop

Buy Speed Boots and Battle Armor. Gear has multiple upgrade levels — each level costs more but provides stronger bonuses.

Items Shop

Buy consumables like Health Packs, Speed Potions, Base Teleports, and Shield Bubbles. These are one-time-use items accessed from the action bar.

🔒 Vault

The Vault is located next to the marketplace. Walk onto the stand pad to open it. The vault lets you protect your cash from death penalties.

How It Works
  • Deposit cash from your wallet into the vault (25%, 50%, or 100%)
  • Withdraw cash from the vault back to your wallet (25%, 50%, or 100%)
  • Vaulted cash is safe from death — when you die, only wallet cash is affected
💡 Strategy: Before entering dangerous zones like Volcano or The Void, deposit most of your cash in the vault. If you die, you only lose what's in your wallet!
🔄 Rebirth

Rebirth resets your progress but unlocks access to the most powerful zones in the game. The top 3 zones require rebirths:

ZoneRebirth Required$/s Range
Ancient RuinsRebirth 1$80-150/s
Sky PlatformRebirth 2$150-300/s
The VoidRebirth 3$300-500/s
What Gets Reset
  • Cash & Vault Cash — back to 0
  • All brainrots — your collection is cleared
  • Zone unlocks — must re-purchase them
  • Base floors — reset to Floor 1
  • Weapons & Gear — must re-purchase
What You Keep
  • Rebirth count — permanently tracked, unlocking new zones
  • Experience — you'll progress much faster knowing the optimal routes
⚠️ Rebirth is permanent! Make sure you're ready. Visit the red reset pad in the marketplace to rebirth.

💡 Tips & Tricks
Early Game ($0 - $5,000)
  • Claim a base immediately — you need it to deposit brainrots
  • Farm Beach and Meadow — they're free and safe (fewer PVP players)
  • Deposit fast, don't hoard — a dead player drops their carried brainrot
  • Buy Floor 2 ($1,000) — unlocks Forest and Jungle zones
Mid Game ($5K - $50K)
  • Buy a Pistol ($1,000) — cheap defense against other players
  • Unlock Cave or Crystal Cove ($8,000 each) — big $/s jump to Epic tier
  • Vault your cash — before going to dangerous zones, deposit in the Vault
  • Speed Boots level 1 ($3,000) — huge quality of life for running between zones and base
  • Floor 3 ($5,000) — unlocks Boss Arena which has Legendary brainrots
Late Game ($50K+)
  • Upgrade to Rifle or Shotgun — much better for PVP in contested zones
  • Invest in Armor — survivability is key when everyone has weapons
  • Floor 4 ($20,000) — unlocks Volcano, Ancient Ruins, Sky Platform, and The Void
  • Vault aggressively before rebirth — you lose everything, so spend it all first
  • Use Shield + Base Teleport combo — shield up, grab a valuable brainrot, teleport home
PVP Tips
  • Check the Players tab in the Inventory — see who's carrying a brainrot and how much cash they have
  • Target players carrying brainrots — they're worth killing because they drop the brainrot
  • Shotgun is king up close — 95 damage can two-shot most players
  • Always keep a Health Pack — $800 to heal to full can save thousands in death penalties
